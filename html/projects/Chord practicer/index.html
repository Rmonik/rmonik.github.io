Tips

  • I've created this tool to help me train every possible chord instantly without relying on muscle memory from familiar songs or circle-of-fifths runs.
  • You can set the BPM on your metronome to the value next to the timer box to match the tempo. Starting it a beat late works best to give you time to figure out the chord. (press a key or click to restart the timer)
  • Avoid just playing the triad in a closed position. Playing the bass in the left hand, and the third and seventh (+ extensions) and omitting the fifth in the right hand is a good way to learn the chords in a more open voicing.
  • Another good way is by playing walking bass patterns in the left hand playing rootless voicings in the right hand.
  • When using scale mode, just playing II-V-Is can help you figure them out faster.
